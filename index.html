About Us

HR Telkom is a vital department within Telkom, playing a crucial role in managing the organization's most valuable asset - its people. As the Human Resources team, they are dedicated to ensuring the recruitment, development, and overall well-being of the employees.

Telkom Jambi is a human resource agency based in Jambi. Our mission is to connect talented individuals with companies in need of their expertise. We pride ourselves on our ability to provide customized solutions to our clients' staffing needs, while also offering career guidance and development to our candidates. Let us help you find your next great opportunity.
